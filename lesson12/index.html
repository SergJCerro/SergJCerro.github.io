The Belize Chamber of Commerce

From each member to every program and volunteer, in Belize or the region, we are shaping the future of living and doing business here, the place we call home.

...brings business and community together to accomplish great things. We are a membership organization with an overarching purpose of speaking out on behalf of business, strengthening the vibrancy of our regional economy and helping businesses grow.

...is more than a membership organization—it's the synergistic core of our community that inspires people, empowers business, and develops a region.
